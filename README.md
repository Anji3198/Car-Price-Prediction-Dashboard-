# Car-Price-Prediction-Dashboard-
1. **Objective**:
To analyze car-related data (brand, model, year, mileage, fuel type, transmission, etc.) and build a dashboard that helps stakeholders understand pricing trends, identify value drivers, and predict car prices.

2. **Dataset**
- Source: CSV file containing car listings and attributes from Kaggle.
- Key Columns: Brand, Model, Year, Mileage, Fuel Type, Transmission, Engine Size, Price
- Size: Large dataset with multiple records (cleaned and transformed for analysis)

3. **Tools & Techniques**
- Power BI: Dashboard creation, DAX measures, calculated columns
- Excel: Initial data cleaning and exploration
- DAX Formulas: For KPIs like average price, car age, price per km, segmentation
- Visualization Types: Bar charts, scatter plots, pie charts, KPI cards, slicers

4. **Key Features of Dashboard****
- Price distribution across brands and models
- Price vs. mileage trend analysis
- Average price by fuel type and transmission
- Car age impact on price
- Top 10 most expensive cars
- Filters for brand, year, fuel type, transmission

5. **Business Insights****
- Identified which brands retain value better
- Highlighted depreciation trends with mileage and age
- Segmented cars into Budget, Mid-range, Premium, and Luxury categories
- Provided KPIs for quick decision-making (average price, % automatic cars, etc.)

6. **Outcome****
- Delivered an interactive dashboard that enables business users to:
- Compare car prices across categories
- Predict approximate car value based on attributes
- Support pricing strategy and customer decision-making
